Drone Case

The Drone Case project was focused on designing a durable, waterproof enclosure to protect sensitive drone equipment during transport and field operations. This case ensures safety, reliability, and ease of use for professional drone users.

Key Features

  • Material: Lightweight, waterproof polymer for ultimate protection.
  • Protection: Shock-absorbing foam interior for secure storage of drones and accessories.
  • Capacity: Customizable compartments for drones, batteries, and other equipment.
  • Weight: 3 kg, making it portable yet durable.
