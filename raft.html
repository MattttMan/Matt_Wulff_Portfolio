Raft Project

The Raft Project demonstrates my resourcefulness and problem-solving skills. The raft was built for fishing and exploring waterways, using repurposed materials to ensure a cost-effective and functional design.

Design and Build Process

  • Base: Constructed using my siblings old barn 2x4's and old barrels
  • Structure: Reinforced with wood planks for stability and strength
  • Purpose: Built to support fishing gear and withstand extended use in moderate waters

Performance Metrics

  • Weight Capacity: 150 kg
  • Dimensions: 2.5 m x 1.5 m
  • Materials: Recycled wood, Deck screws, and Desiel barrels
  • Build Time: 2 weeks
