Who Am I?

I'm Matt—a Mechatronics Engineering student who's obsessed with building things. Robots, software, hardware, startups, you name it. I've probably tried to automate it or make it smarter.

My philosophy is simple: learn by doing. Want to understand robotics? Build one. Curious about AI? Train a model. Wondering how something works? Take it apart and rebuild it better.

I've gone from building my first sketchy robotic arm to creating AI security platforms, founding startups, and participating in accelerators. Every project teaches me something new. Every failure is just data.

The dream? Create technology that actually matters. Not just cool for cool's sake—but solutions that solve real problems and improve people's lives.
