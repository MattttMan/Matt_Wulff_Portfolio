Nutrium: Nutrition Management

Professional Software for Nutritionists

Nutrium is a comprehensive nutrition management platform designed specifically for nutritionists and dietitians. It streamlines client management, meal planning, progress tracking, and nutritional analysis to help professionals deliver personalized nutrition care more efficiently and effectively.

Platform Overview

Nutrium addresses the complex needs of nutrition professionals by providing:

  • Centralized client database management
  • Customizable meal planning tools
  • Nutritional analysis and tracking
  • Progress monitoring and reporting
  • Communication tools for client engagement
  • Evidence-based nutrition guidelines
