Eloquently App

The Eloquently App is an iOS application designed to help users improve their public speaking skills through AI-powered feedback. With real-time analysis and actionable insights, it provides an intuitive platform for building confidence and eloquence.

Key Features

  • AI Feedback: Real-time analysis of speech clarity, word usage, and pacing.
  • Score: Offers performance scores to track improvement over time.
  • User-Friendly: Intuitive design and clean interface.
  • Software: Developed with Swift and integrated with ChatGPT AI APIs.
