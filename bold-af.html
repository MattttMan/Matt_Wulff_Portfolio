Bold AF

Break free from stress. Build unshakeable confidence.

Bold AF is an iOS app that helps people escape anxiety and build real-world confidence through daily challenges, streak tracking, and an AI coach. Built from personal experience — stress shouldn't win.

🔥

Daily Challenges

Legendary, hard, and normal-mode challenges that push you outside your comfort zone every day.

📈

Confidence Tracking

Visual progress meter that grows as you complete challenges and build streaks.

🤖

AI Coach — Bestie

An AI companion that keeps you accountable, celebrates wins, and helps you push through hard days.

⚡

Streak System

150-day streaks, panic button for tough moments, and a timer to keep momentum going.

Tech Stack

  • Platform: iOS (Swift / SwiftUI)
  • AI: Claude API — powers the Bestie coach and challenge generation
  • Backend: Firebase (auth, Firestore, analytics)
  • Monetization: RevenueCat subscriptions — Premium tier
  • Distribution: App Store

Status

  • Stage: Live on the App Store
  • Users: Active daily users with 150+ day streaks
  • Role: Solo founder — design, development, marketing
